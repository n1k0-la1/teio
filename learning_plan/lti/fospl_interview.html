II. Interview questions ::>-<::

  • Haiii! My name is Niko! Thank you so much for letting me meet with you today! ^^
  • Before we start, is it okay if I record this interview for my personal reference? Alright, thank you!
  • To start us off, can you introduce yourself a little? How'd you get into this field?
  • Have you worked/Where've you worked at before the FOSPL? Can you tell me a little about your experiences there?
  • How do you feel the experiences you've had there've helped you with your career? What skills and expertise have you built that you feel is the most helpful for you?
  • Are there any skills you've learned outside of your career that you feel are vital?
  • Why did you end up deciding to join the FOSPL?
  • What was the most exciting parts when you first joined? What about now?
  • How're the positions you've had before different to the one you have now?
  • What's your usual day-to-day like at the FOSPL?
  • What's your favorite part of your job? What's more difficult?
  • Looking back, would you do anything differently if you had to start from scratch again?
  • Do you feel like the environment of the field is different from when you first started? How so?
  • Do you feel like it's harder to enter now than before? What experience/education did you have coming in, and what experience/education do you feel people should have coming in now?
  • [checks clock]
  • Ah, I wouldn't want to cut into your time so we should start wrapping up; do you know anyone else in the field who'd be interested in doing an interview? Could I please have their contact information?
  • Alright, thank you so much! Have a nice day!
